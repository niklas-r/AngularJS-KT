Introduction

Development started by Miško Hevery and Adam Abrons in 2009. Later on Igor Minár and Vojta Jína joined the project.

Projektet började som en betallösning. Planen var att utvecklare enkelt skulle kunna hosta sin data i JSON-form genom GetAngular.com för att underlätta utvecklingen av webbapplikationer. Valde dock att släppa som open source.

AngularJS philosophy

Declarative DOM. AngularJS loves semantic markup and let's you extend it with the use of "directives".

Testing. Equally important as the application writing.

Decouple client from server. This allows development work to progress in parallel, and allows for reuse of both sides.
